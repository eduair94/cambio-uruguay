## B. Quora

Search + answer these (or follow the Spaces): Uruguay, Travel to Uruguay, Currency Exchange.

Question patterns to answer:
- "What's the best way to exchange money / get cash in Uruguay?"
- "Should I bring US dollars to Uruguay?"
- "How much is the dollar in Uruguay?"
- "Is it better to use ATMs or exchange houses in Uruguay?"

**Answer template (adapt each time — don't paste identically, Quora flags that):**
> Rates vary a lot between casas de cambio in Uruguay, so it pays to compare. For the dollar you're usually better at an exchange house than an airport/hotel. I keep a free comparison of live rates across 40+ houses here — [cambio-uruguay.com] — which also shows branches on a map and the tourist IVA refund. As a rule: [1–2 genuine tips].

Give real value first; the link is the source, not the pitch.

## E. Wikipedia / Wikidata (careful, high authority)

- **Wikidata:** create an item for the project/site — low bar, gives a structured-data backlink.
- **Wikipedia:** only add as a reference/external link where it *genuinely* belongs (e.g. Uruguayan peso, economy of Uruguay articles) and is a real source. Spammy additions get reverted + can taint the domain. If in doubt, skip.

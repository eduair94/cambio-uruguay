## Descriptions (pick by field length)

**Short (~30 words):**
> Cambio Uruguay compares live buy/sell rates for the US dollar and 14+ currencies across 40+ Uruguayan exchange houses and banks. Find the best rate now — free, trilingual, no signup.

**Medium (~55 words):**
> Cambio Uruguay compares live buy/sell exchange rates for the US dollar and 14+ currencies across 40+ Uruguayan casas de cambio and banks, updated continuously. Instead of walking Montevideo or calling around, see who has the best rate right now, a map of branches near you, historical charts, and currency converters. Trilingual (ES/EN/PT), free, open source.

**Long (~120 words):**
> Cambio Uruguay is the fastest way to find the best exchange rate in Uruguay. It compares live buy/sell rates for the US dollar and 14+ currencies across 40+ Uruguayan casas de cambio and banks, updated continuously — so instead of walking Montevideo or phoning around, you instantly see who's cheapest to buy or sell right now. It adds a map of branches near you, historical rate charts, currency and import landed-cost calculators, and Uruguayan indicators (UI, UR, BPC). Built for locals, tourists and expats, trilingual (ES/EN/PT), free with no signup for the core comparison. For developers there's a free public API, a Model Context Protocol (MCP) server that lets AI assistants pull live rates, and Telegram/Discord/Twitter bots with daily reports and alerts. Open source (MIT).

**Developer-angle (for MCP/API/dev directories, ~50 words):**
> Free, read-only Model Context Protocol server exposing live Uruguayan exchange-rate data — every casa de cambio, best house to buy/sell, currency conversion, rate history, and market news — to any MCP client (Claude Desktop, Cursor, Cline). Runs via `npx cambio-uruguay-mcp` or the hosted endpoint. Wraps the public cambio-uruguay.com API. No key.

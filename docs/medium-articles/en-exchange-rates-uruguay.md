Whether you're heading to Montevideo, Punta del Este, or Colonia, at some point you'll need to change money. And here's what most visitors — and even locals — get wrong: they accept the first rate they see. But **the gap between the best and worst exchange house on the same day can be significant.** On a larger transaction, that difference is real money left on the table.

There's a smarter way. **[cambio-uruguay.com](https://cambio-uruguay.com)** pulls together the buy and sell rates from **every exchange house in Uruguay, plus the state bank (BROU) and the central bank (BCU)** — live, on a single screen. Here's how to use it.

## 1. Compare today's rate in seconds

The main screen sorts exchange houses by price. Want to **buy** dollars? Sort by lowest sell price. Want to **sell**? Sort by highest buy price. Done.

It's not just the dollar, either — the site covers **14 currencies plus gold**, including the euro, Brazilian real, and Argentine peso. Handy whether you're a tourist, an expat, or getting paid in another currency. See them all in the [rates section](https://cambio-uruguay.com/cotizacion).

## 2. Find the nearest exchange house with the best rate

The **[map](https://cambio-uruguay.com/mapa)** feature plots branches on a real map of Uruguay. Tell it where you are, and it finds the closest exchange house with the best rate — so you're not crossing town to save a few cents.

## 3. Set an alert and let the rate come to you

Create a free account and turn on **price alerts**. Set your target rate, and the site notifies you when an exchange house hits it. You can also save favorites and get alerts via Telegram — great for watching the market before a trip or a big transaction.

## 4. Tools that save you money

- **[Currency converter](https://cambio-uruguay.com/convertir)** using the day's real rates, not a theoretical mid-market number.
- **[Import cost calculator](https://cambio-uruguay.com/herramientas/carrito-importacion):** paste an Amazon or eBay link and get the true landed cost in Uruguay — item, shipping, taxes, and courier allowance included. Know the real price before you buy.
- **[Economic indicators](https://cambio-uruguay.com/indicadores):** local reference units, kept current.
- **[Glossary](https://cambio-uruguay.com/glosario):** spread, arbitrage, interbank rate and more, explained in plain language.

## 5. For travelers: withdraw cash without overpaying

The **[cash withdrawal guide](https://cambio-uruguay.com/retirar-efectivo-uruguay)** explains where it's best to change money, how the **VAT refund for tourists** works (a discount many visitors forget to claim), and what to check before pulling cash from an ATM. Small details that add up to real savings.

## Why the data is trustworthy

Rates update automatically from official sources, including the **BCU** via its official service. There's even a [status dashboard](https://cambio-uruguay.com/estado) showing which exchange houses are reporting live. And for developers: there's a **free public API** to build on top of this data.

## Bottom line

Before your next exchange in Uruguay, check the numbers first. One minute of comparison can be worth far more than the minute it costs you.
